Access Controls Worksheet

Note(s) Issue(s) Recommendation(s)

● All employees had unrestricted access to every file on the network, regardless of their role.

● Sensitive files (e.g., financial documents, client data) were stored in shared directories accessible to all employees.

● The absence of role-based access control (RBAC) allowed users to access sensitive data not relevant to their job functions.

● Unauthorized access to sensitive files was possible because employees, regardless of their position, had unrestricted access.

● Implement role-based access control (RBAC) to ensure that only employees who need access to specific files have the necessary permissions.

● Establish least privilege access policies, ensuring that users only have access to files necessary for their specific job role.

● Regularly audit access controls to verify that employees’ access levels are appropriate and up-to-date.

● Data classification should be implemented, ensuring sensitive information is stored in more secure, restricted-access locations.
