Date: July 23, 2024

Entry: #1

Description

Documenting a cybersecurity incident.

Tool(s) Used

  • Wireshark: For network monitoring
  • SIEM Platform: For incident logging and alerts

The 5 W's

  • Who: An external group of hackers exploiting weak access control policies and phishing tactics.
  • What: A potential data breach from unauthorized access to critical systems via phishing and credential theft.
  • Where: At Source Technology
  • When: July 23, 2024, at 9:00 a.m.
  • Why: The attackers exploited a phishing vulnerability where an employee unknowingly clicked on a malicious link, allowing the attackers to steal credentials and access internal systems. The breach was likely aimed at either data theft or deploying further exploits to access sensitive business data.

Additional Notes

  1. Implementing a more robust email filtering system to catch phishing attempts before reaching employees.
  2. Enhancing user access controls to ensure critical data is better protected by limiting unnecessary access.
  3. Conducting frequent phishing simulations and training to ensure employees recognize phishing tactics.

Incident Response Steps Taken

  1. Identification: An employee reported unusual system behavior, including strange email alerts and unauthorized file access attempts.
  2. Containment: Immediately blocked the phishing email source and isolated affected systems to prevent lateral movement.
  3. Eradication: Conducted a thorough investigation of logs and systems, confirmed that the breach was due to credential theft from phishing, and removed all unauthorized access points.
  4. Recovery: Restored systems from backups, ensuring no ransomware or malware was present in the recovered data.
